about the project
A mixed-use/Corporate High-rise building located in San Mateo, Rizal wherein the company OSCORP, will be constructing their new facility which will house research and development facilities, rcreational, and residential spaces.

The city is envisioned to be commercialized in the future.
➜building approach images
design approach
The company, OSCORP, wishes for a green and sustainable building and a product development facility. As a design approach the structure will be designed wherein it transitions into a useful and productive work area with unique and engaging system for its users. The building also transitions along with its surroundings as the area is to be developed in the future.
➜interior perspectives
design goal
The goal is to design a mixed-use building which addresses the needs and requirements of the company and its users while incorporating green and sustainable elements.
